This B2B Hotel Partnership Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on the date mentioned above by and between:

ShamBit Travels, a company incorporated under the laws of India, having its principal place of business at [ShamBit Address] (hereinafter referred to as the "Company"),

AND

[Hotel/Legal Counterparty Name], having its principal place of business at [Hotel Address] (hereinafter referred to as the "Hotel Partner").

1. Scope of Agreement

1.1 The Hotel Partner agrees to provide accommodation services, including room-wise and/or global (bulk) inventory, to the Company for further distribution to B2B travel agents and partners.

1.2 This Agreement governs the pricing modes, margins, tax applications, and general terms under which the Hotel Partner's inventory will be distributed via the ShamBit platform.

2. Commercial Terms & Pricing

2.1 Pricing Mode: The applicable pricing mode (Room-wise, Global, or Both) shall be explicitly defined in the active digital contract on the ShamBit Admin platform.

2.2 Commissions & Margins: The Hotel Partner agrees to the predefined Net Rates. The ShamBit profit margin and B2B Agent TAC (Total Agency Commission) are governed by the digital contract settings and added on top of the Hotel Net Rate.

2.3 Free of Cost (FOC) Rooms: Any FOC policies (e.g., 1 complimentary room for every 15 booked) shall be honored as per the digitally recorded terms.

3. Payment & Settlement

3.1 All settlements will be processed based on the 'Hotel Net' rate as agreed in the digital system.

3.2 Tax (GST) applications, whether pre-tax or post-tax, must comply with the rates specified in the digital agreement and applicable Indian taxation laws.

4. Term & Termination

4.1 This Agreement shall commence on the "Effective From" date and remain valid until the "Effective To" date as recorded digitally.

4.2 Either party may terminate this agreement with a 30-day prior written notice. However, all bookings confirmed prior to termination must be honored.

5. Amendments

5.1 Any changes to pricing or commercial terms require a formal "Amendment" triggered via the ShamBit Admin portal, followed by a newly signed addendum.

6. Dispute Resolution & Governing Law

6.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

6.2 Any disputes arising out of this agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in [City, State].
